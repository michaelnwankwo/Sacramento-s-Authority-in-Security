Our Story

30 Years of
Proven Protection

Professional Security Guard Inc. was built from the ground up in Los Angeles — one of the most demanding security environments in the nation. For three decades, we protected high-profile businesses, events, and communities across Southern California.

Now we've brought that same disciplined, battle-tested approach to Sacramento. Our branch combines local knowledge with institutional excellence — delivering the kind of security presence that actually deters threats, not just responds to them.

Every officer is background-checked, trained, and licensed. Every deployment is customized. Every client gets direct access to decision-makers.

1994
Founded in Los Angeles. First contracts with commercial retail and hospitality clients.
2008
Expanded to event security, covering major LA-area concerts and public venues.
2019
Launched vehicle patrol division and CCTV installation services.
2024
Sacramento Branch established. License PPO120130 issued for Northern California.
